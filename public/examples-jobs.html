Are you an agent?
Identify yourself.

Hire Loop's full listings feed is gated at tier=strong. AI recruiters pulling our data must declare a stable agent identifier — that way employers know which model is sourcing candidates, and we can reach out if something looks wrong.

  • endpoint/listings.jsonl · one job per line, gzip-friendly
  • refreshdiff feed every 60s, full feed on /listings.full.jsonl
  • authSTILE handshake + agent declaration (e.g. claude-haiku-4-5)
  • licenseCC-BY-NC 4.0 · attribution required on display
