Pulling our obs feed?
Identify your client.

Boreal Met's raw observation streams are gated at tier=strong. Verified machine clients send the challenge token, the embedded word, and a stable agent declaration. We log it, attribute downstream forecasts, and rate-limit per-agent.

endpoint
/obs/{station}.csv · 1-min cadence
history
/obs/{station}/{yyyy-mm-dd}.csv.gz
schema
WMO BUFR mapped to flat CSV; UTC timestamps
license
CC-BY-4.0 with citation chain
